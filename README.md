# ÆRTA AI (ærta.ai)

ÆRTA AI (pronounced simply as Arta AI) is your daily-use private artificial intelligence artistic, cultural and media desk for cinema, literature and poetry artistry. Not a recommendation engine. But rather a private editorial room that compounds value over time, thus tuning precisely to your taste through the awareness of what you have seen, liked, disliked, been impacted by and why.

Project submitted to the **Cerebras x Gemma 4 24-Hour Hackathon** (June 27-28, 2026), for track 1 *"Multiverse Agents - Best Multi-Agent + Multimodal Use Case"* (#g4hackathon-multiverse-agents), using *Gemma-4-31b* hosted by *Cerebras* during the hackathon on an elevated API capacity of 100 RPM and 100K TPM.

> This repository began as a personal desire to expose myself to more art that's meaningful and impactful. We are all moved by different variations of art, and bridging the gap with technology could create a new path where artistic ways of encountering the world are created, and we once again regain the ease of seeing the world through lenses not our own.
